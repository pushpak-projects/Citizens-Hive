PAY PER HOUR

The Citizens Hive platform offers options for seniors to pay the caregivers on an hourly basis.

The hourly charge is priced at $14/hr.






MOVE-IN WITH CAREGIVER

The Citizens Hive platform offers seniors the service to move in with a caregiver for a scheduled period of time. The senior will recieve care at the caregiver's home.

The service is priced at $3000/month



MOVE-IN WITH SENIOR

The Citizens Hive platform offers seniors the service to pay for a caregiver to provide care at the senior's home.

The service is priced at $2200/month
